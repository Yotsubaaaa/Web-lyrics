ABOUT THIS SONG



"It's Not Like I Like You!!" is a popular song created by STATIC-P and Amree in 2016, which gained its fame through its use in various animated music videos (AMVs) and animatics. The song's lyrics depict the relationship between a boy and a girl, where the girl exhibits a tsundere personality trait. In anime and sometimes in real life, people who have a tsundere personality initially portray a cold, harsh attitude to someone but have a warm, friendly, and kind personality deep down.
