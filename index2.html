---RED VELVET---

A South Korean female group called Red Velvet has five members, starting with the oldest, named Irene, followed by Seulgi, Wendy, Joy, and Yeri. Under SM Entertainment, the group made its debut on August 1st, 2014. Pastel Coral serves as their official fandom color, and they have a fanbase called ReVeluv. They became well-known thanks to their captivating voice and blinding light visuals, which led to them performing in North Korea and around the world!

The song "Psycho," which expresses what it was like to be in an odd relationship, was written and co-produced by Kenzie, a Korean singer, along with Druski, EJAE, and a Swedish musician by the name of Cazzi Opeia. On December 23, 2019, Red Velvet's song "Psycho" was released. It has received 379, 667, 541 Spotify plays under the album "The ReVe Festival Finale," where it is currently their number-one hit.
